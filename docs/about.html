About Me

The name Phased Star has no real meaning, I belive it was the coolest username I could think of when making my Instagram account. My art has been described as "Harry Who meeting Mexican realism". This definitly came as a shock to myself, considering my art career began with tracing manga art. As of reacently, my focus has been towards, my major, graphic design and bold digital pieces.

About My Art

The process for my pieces typically begins with a sketch so vauge, only I can really tell what it is. From there I do many concept sketches until I get every detail mapped out for the final product.
